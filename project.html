CMHC Retrofit Grant Application & Implementation

CMHC Retrofit Grant Application & Implementation

Worked with the client board of directors, the Co-operative Housing Federation of Canada (CHF) and an engineering firm to complete a Building Condition Assessment of the client’s 12 duplexes (24 units). The project involved coordinating contractor selection, organizing a site visit to 9/12 duplexes, responding to any questions about previous maintenance for engineers including procuring documents. Also, completing a review of the BCA to ensure it met the standards of both the CHF and the CMHC Agency for Co-operative Housing so it could be used to further develop a long-term Asset Management Plan and related budget.
