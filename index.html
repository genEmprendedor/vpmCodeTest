About

As co-op and condominuim residents, Viking Property Management Ltd. was formed with a focus on providing professional and reliable property management and maintenance that is simple and hassle free for owners, members and tenants

Years of experience working with housing co-operatives and condominiums means we are well versed in all standard pertinent bylaws, as well as the Alberta Co-operative’s Act, Alberta’s Condominium Property Act and Alberta’s Private Information Protection Act (PIPA)

Our company has over 20 years expereince in property and construction management and have provided services for projects costing from hundreds to millions of dollars.

We are a full-service operation–from landscaping and drywall to roofs and foundations Viking has your property covered. We also offer an extensive range of administration and financial services such as bookeeping, audit preperation and more.
